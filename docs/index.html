Baseball Drills

Hitting and Fielding Drills for All Age Groups

Introduction

This book is a practical guide for baseball/ softball coaches and players who want a single reference for skill development. Each chapter organizes drills by skill area and includes descriptions, key teaching points, and video examples.

Use this book as:

  • A practice design tool — pick drills based on your focus for the day.
  • A reference guide — find specific drills for mechanics or strategy.
  • A training progression — follow chapters in order to build complete fundamentals.

Equipment & Safety Notes

Before starting any drill:

  • Inspect equipment for safety and wear.
  • Make sure throwing and hitting zones are clear.
  • Emphasize proper warm-up routines and stretching before drills.
  • Encourage hydration and recovery time.

Warm-up Recommendation

Begin each practice with:

  1. Dynamic warm-up (5–10 minutes) — jog, lunges, arm circles, mobility.
  2. Throwing progression (10-20 minutes) — short toss to long toss.
  3. Skill warm-up/ Dailies (5-10 minutes) — short, focused fundamental drill.
